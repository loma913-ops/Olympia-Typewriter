Olympia Typewriter is a typeface that rescues the beauty of mid-century mechanical typography. Inspired by the "Elite" typeface of the classic Olympia SM3 manual typewriter, the goal is to bring the tactile soul of the typewriter into the digital age.

As the era of mechanical typewriters recedes, this project aims to preserve the specific character and geometric precision of the Olympia Elite design. By digitizing these forms, the project ensures that the unique aesthetic of typebars striking paper remains accessible under a free, libre and open source license, the SIL Open Font License.
